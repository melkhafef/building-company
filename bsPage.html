About

ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Founded in 1963 by the late Charles B. West III, the grandson of a prominent Greenville builder and architect, Custom Building Company boasts a heritage in design and construction that spans five generations. West led Custom Building Company into the commercial construction industry by the late 1960s. As the company expanded, family legacy continued with West’s son Pete, who worked in business from his teen years through college. After his father’s passing in 1981, Pete assumed the company’s helm, reincorporating in 1985 with an unlimited general contractor’s license. With distinctive building designs and exceptional construction techniques, the business flourished. Today, Custom Building Company’s team leverages the company’s decades of construction experience to deliver on a reputation for excellence now and well into the future.

Why Choose Custom Building Company
When it’s time to start your construction project, you need a company that builds on experience and stands by its product. You seek a company that is more concerned with having a solid reputation than having a flashy image. You choose a company that offers the ideal combination of quality, service, and price. We believe that company is Custom Building Company.
